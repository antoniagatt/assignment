TEA TIME PACKAGE DESIGN

'Dapple' is a Package Design Project aimed at designing teatime products.

The brand itself represents the time on sits down to have tea, when the sun starts to go down and 'dappled' light forms.

Packaging Design Project, 2020

The aim of the project was to create a cluster-package that includes, jam, tea and cookies.
